Let's Play Dice!!!!

  1. Each player has a chance to play 6 consecutive times at a time
  2. If a player roles the dice to 1, their score is reset to zero and their opponent has an opportunity to play
  3. A player is allowed to Hold. Their current score is reset to their Total Score
  4. The first player to score 100 points wins!!!!
